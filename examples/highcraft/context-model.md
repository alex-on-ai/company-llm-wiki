## 6. Voice

**Tone:**
Confident, dry, exact. A senior builder explaining things plainly — direct, a little deadpan, never salesy.

**How we do NOT sound:**
Never small, new, scrappy, or boutique. No hype, no corporate sheen, no exclamation marks, no emoji, no em dashes.

**Writing patterns:**
- A number beats an adjective ("shipped in two weeks", not "fast")
- Show, don't claim: proof first, assertion never
- Dry aside every few hundred words; punches at ourselves or physics, never at the reader

**Sticker phrases:**
1. "Show, don't claim."
2. "A working prototype in two weeks, not a slide deck."
3. "Software that fits the workflow you already have."

**Forbidden words:**
leverage · unlock · seamless · world-class · "comprehensive solution" · transformative · cutting-edge · passionate · "Whether you're X, Y, or Z" openers · any copy that shrinks the company
